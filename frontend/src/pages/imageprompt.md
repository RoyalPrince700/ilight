# Image Generation Prompts

Use these prompts in Gemini to generate clean, modern, realistic images for the website. Keep the overall visual style consistent across all images: professional solar-energy brand photography, realistic lighting, premium but practical feel, African/Nigerian context where appropriate, sharp details, natural colors, no text, no watermark, no logo overlay, no distorted hands, no extra limbs.
